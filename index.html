Brief Biography

Born and raised in Jacksonville, Florida, I received both my B.A. and M.A. degrees very close to home at the University of North Florida in Jacksonville. I have since moved to the frigid North (at least at the time I'm writing this) of Pennsylvania, which is a far cry from the sunny, blisteringly hot Florida I am accustomed to, in an attempt to acquire at long last my Ph.D. in English Literature and Criticism at the Indiana University of Pennsylvania.
