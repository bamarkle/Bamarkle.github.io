Select a different media format you could potentially use for your evaluation essay that's NOT words on a paper.

1) Identify the new media

2) Identify at least two tools you could use to help create the essay (e.g., if you're doing a podcast you might identify Adobe Audition and Apple GarageBand)

3) List the affordances and constraints of the new media (e.g., you can hear things, but you can't see things, etc.)

4) Copy/paste some locations online that you can use for resources/help about your media (forums, webpages, digital manuals, etc.)

5) Explain why you are thinking about using this media and what you hope it will bring to the project that pen and paper would be lacking.
